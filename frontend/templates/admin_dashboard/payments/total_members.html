Member Management Suggestions

  • Verification of Registration Forms: Melissa suggests that the "Manage Users" section could be used for verifying uploaded registration forms. This implies a workflow where users upload their registration forms, and administrators use the system to check and approve them.
  • Main System Goal: The primary goal of the system is to collect payments.
  • List of Members per Semester:
    • The list of members should be organized per semester.
    • Each semester's list should indicate the payment status of each member (paid or unpaid).
    • The list should include full names.
  • Excel-like Display: Melissa suggests displaying the member data in an Excel-like format, with columns for each semester and indicators for payment status.
  • Fourth-Year Clearance: Membership fees don't have a deadline, but 4th-year students cannot get their clearance signed if they haven't paid all their dues for all semesters.
